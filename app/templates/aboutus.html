It is said that man was once but a piece of clay till a soul
was breathed in him. Then he was commanded to breathe. Breathe.
And thus he did.

He breathed.

But man is a breathless being, constantly short of breath. Breathe.
Man, lost in the physical, lost in the body, lost in the mud. Breathe.

When will man exercise his spirit, acknowledge his soul? When will man
find himself in others, and love others to love himself? He will do so when
he breathes. So Breathe. For then there will be Rizz. So let there be Rizz.

- The Grand Rizzard, Ahmed Alowmari
